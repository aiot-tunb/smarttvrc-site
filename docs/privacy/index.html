Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Remote SmartTV is a local-only TV remote control app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how the app operates.

1Information We Collect

Remote SmartTV does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data — including TV connection details, app settings, and PIN — is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

2How We Use Information

TV Control

TV IP addresses and pairing credentials are stored locally to establish connections. This data is not shared with any third party.

Voice Search

Microphone audio is streamed directly to your TV in real time and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server.

App Lock

Your PIN is stored securely on your device using the system keychain. Biometric authentication uses your device's built-in system and is never accessed by the app.

3Permissions

  • Local Network — Required to discover and connect to Smart TVs on your local Wi-Fi network.
  • Microphone — Used only during active voice search sessions. Audio is streamed directly to your TV.
  • Face ID / Fingerprint — Used to unlock the app when App Lock is enabled. Biometric data is managed entirely by the device OS.

4Data Storage & Security

All app data is stored on your device only. We use the system keychain for sensitive data (PIN, pairing keys). No data is transmitted to our servers.

5Third-Party Services

The app uses Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics to receive anonymous crash reports and usage statistics. These services do not collect personally identifiable information.

6Children's Privacy

Remote SmartTV does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not require account registration or any personal data.

7Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
